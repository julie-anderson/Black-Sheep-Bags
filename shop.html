Amanda Backpack

A fun backpack design with a folded top and split strap is perfect for everyday use. Approx. finished size is 6 ½” X 7” X 12″ & it uses 3 skeins of Cascade 220.

Booga Bag

Special Edition!! Now includes directions for the Baby Booga Bag which requires only 2 skeins of Noro Kureyon and measures approx. 3 ½” X 6 ½” X 7 ½”. The original Booga Bag uses 3 skeins of Noro Kureyon and measures approx. 4 ½” X 7 ½” X 8 ½”.

more info

Bigger & Biggest Booga Bag

The Bigger Bag is a larger version of the popular Booga Bag. The pattern includes 2 sizes. The Bigger Booga Bag uses 3 skeins of Noro Kureyon and 2 skeins of Cascade 220 while the Biggest Booga Bag takes 4 skeins of Noro Kureyon and 2 skeins of Cascade 220. The finished sizes are approx. 10″ X 6″ X 11″ and 12″ X 6″ x 11″.

more info

Carroll Bag

The Carroll Bag is a medium sized bag, perfect as an everyday handbag or a medium sized project bag. I think it would also make a good spindle bag! The Kureyon version uses 4 skeins and the bag made with Cascade 220 takes 2 skeins. The finished size is approx. 9″ X 3″ X 11″.

more info

Dorothy Bag

The Dorothy Bag is a fold over style handbag that will keep your items secure. If you’ve been wanting to try entrelac, this is the bag for you! It takes 4 skeins of Noro Kureyon and a beautiful button of your choice. The finished size is approx. 10″ X 2″ X 10″ when folded.

more info

Elizabeth Bag

A grown up version of the Sophie, this stylish bag has more sophisticated shaping and is much larger! It uses 3 skeins of Cascade 220 and measures approx. 12″ at the base & 9″ at the top X 6″ X 11″.

more info

Heidi Tote

A tote designed with sturdy wrap around handles which give it the strength to hold a heavy load. This roomy bag uses 5 skeins of Cascade 220 and measures approx. 12″ X 3 ½” X 16″.

more info

Kristina Bag

A great introduction to two color knitting! The Kristina Bag uses one skein of Noro Kureyon and one skein of Cascade 220 (2 skeins of Cascade for a longer handle.) Instructions for both the zippered and un-zippered versions are included. Approx. finished size is 8″ X 2″ X 7″ (6 ½” for zippered version). If you are ready for slightly more advanced techniques, this design is for you.

more info

Lily Bag

Designed to be an everyday handbag or a small project tote. The bottom is oval and the sides gently flare out. Approx. finished size is 5″ X 7 ½” at base (11″ at top) X 8″ for bag made with Cascade 220 and 5 ½” X 8 ½” at base (13″ at top) X 9″ for bag made with Noro Kureyon. It uses 2 skeins of Cascade 220 or 4 skeins of Noro Kureyon.

more info

Madeleine Bag

An evening bag that measures approx. 4″ X 3″ X 4″. It uses one skein of Cascade 220 and can be easily completed in a weekend. Great introduction to felting!

Sophie Bag

Cute and quick knit that is fun to embellish. This bag uses 1 skeins of Cascade 220 and measures approx. 8″ at the base X 6″ at the top X 3″ X 6″.
